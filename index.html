Domain Transfer Information

Domain Transfer Process

Once we agree on a price and terms, the domain transfer process will follow these steps:

  • Step 1 - Agreement: We confirm the sale price and payment details. A 50% deposit is required to proceed.
  • Step 2 - Payment: You make the deposit payment via your preferred method (bank transfer, PayPal, or cryptocurrency).
  • Step 3 - Authorization: Once payment is confirmed, I will unlock the domain and request the authorization code (EPP code).
  • Step 4 - Transfer: You receive the EPP code and can initiate the transfer through your domain registrar.
  • Step 5 - Completion: The transfer is processed and finalized. The remaining 50% payment is due upon successful transfer completion.

Transfer Timeline

Total time: 5-7 business days from agreement to full domain ownership transfer.

  • Agreement & Deposit: 1 business day
  • Domain Unlock & EPP Code: 1-2 business days
  • Domain Transfer Processing: 3-5 business days (depends on your registrar)
  • Final Confirmation: 24 hours

The transfer process is secure and follows ICANN (Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers) standards to ensure a smooth and legitimate transaction.
